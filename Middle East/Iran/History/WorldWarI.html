World War I

  • From 1911-14 Russia effectively ruled Iran.
  • However, after the outbreak of the world war, they withdrew and the third Majles was convened.
  • Over the course of the war the country turned into a battle ground between the British, German, Russian, and Turkish armies in what became the Persian campaign.
  • The Persian Cossack Brigade fought with the Russians against the Turkish army.
