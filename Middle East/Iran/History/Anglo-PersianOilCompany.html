Anglo-Persian Oil Company

  • In May 1908, D'Arcy found his first strike of oil in the Middle East.
  • In April, this led to the formation of the Anglo-Persian Oil Company to take over the oil-field concession.
  • This first strike sparked a new gold rush to the Middle East for geopolitical oil interests.
  • By 1914, the British government would become the primary shareholder in the company.
