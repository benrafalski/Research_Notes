Nadir Shah

  • After Nadr expelled the Afghans, he later dethroned Tahmasp
  • He dethroned Tahmasp because he secretly invaded the Turks and was defeated and replaced him with Abbas III and eventually claimed rule for himself in 1736
  • Nadir established Iran's first navy
  • In 1738-39 he captured India in a conquest
  • Nadir eventually became obsessed with treasure and became unhinged
  • After suspecting his own son of plotting against him he had him blinded in 1741
  • He also tried to convert Iran to Sunnism
  • In 1747, he was murdered by a group of his own Afsharid tribesmen and Qajar chiefs for being too obsessed with treasure and conquest.
  • After his death Iran was split among his military commanders, who fought one another for power over Iran.
