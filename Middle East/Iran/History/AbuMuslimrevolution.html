Abu Muslim revolution

  • Abu Muslim was the leader of the Islamic revolution in Khorasan in 747
  • caused by Arab assimilation with Iranians in colonized regions
  • After Muhammad died in 632 Abu Bakr took over as the first caliph
  • By 644 Uthman ibn Affan had risen to become the third caliph but some minority factions wanted a different caliph
  • the Shi'ah wanted Ali, Muhammad's cousin, to become caliph
  • In 656 Uthman was murdered and Ali took over as caliph
  • In 661 Ali was killed by a Kharijite (another Islamic minority sect)
  • After this, the Umayyad dynasty rose to power in Damascus and became the first great Muslim dynasty
  • In 747 Abu Muslim led a revolution in Khorasan due to Arab and Iranian discontent with Umayyad taxes and replaced the Umayyads with the Abbasid caliphate in 750
