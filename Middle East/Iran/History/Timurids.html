Timurids

  • The Muzaffarids controlled Iran until Timur began his conquests.
  • Between 1380-1405 Timur (Tamerlane), a descendant of Genghis Khan, entered Iran
  • He conquered the Jalayirids, beat the Ottomans, and took over Syria.
  • Timur was extremely brutal in his conquest and he eventually died of illness while traveling to China.
  • After Timur died, his dynasty was divided among his two sons.
  • Between 1406-17, one of the sons, Shah Rokh, extended his territory over most of Iran
