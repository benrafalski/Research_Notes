The Amorites

  • From 2000-1600 BC the Amorites (Semitic westerners) migrated and rules Syria and Mesopotamia
  • They were seen as westerners but most likely from Arabia, not Syria
  • They were believed to be one of the causes of the 3rd Ur dynasty
  • During the 1000s BC there was much migration from Arabia to Babylonia and Syria-Palestine, these were the Canaanites
  • The Amorites were a specific group within the Canaanites
  • Hammurabi was the first king of the Amorites in Aleppo and Babylonia from 1792-1750 BC
  • Hammurabi is best known for his set of laws known as the Hammurabi code and bringing Mesopotaimia again under a single rule
  • He conquered Uruk, Isin, and all of Mesopotaimia, and his later rule was marked by continuous war
  • Hammurabi also captures Mari from the local king Zimrilim
