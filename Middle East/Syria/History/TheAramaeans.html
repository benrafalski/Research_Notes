The Aramaeans

  • It is said that the Aramaeans lived in norther Syria in the 1600s BC
  • They were said to originally be know as the Akhlame, but this is still debated
  • By 1000 BC, they had formed the state of Bit-Adini, holding Anatolia and northern Syria up to the Anti-Lebanon mountains
  • They established their principle kingdom in Damascus and began to fight with the Israelites in Jerusalem
  • The also turned to the Assyrians next and became mortal enemies as the Aramaeans lauched invasions on the Assyrian kings
  • In 720 BC, the Aramaean kingdom was ended by Sargon II of Assyria
