King Suppiluliumas

  • In 1380 BC, Suppiluliumas I took over as king of the Hittite empire
  • Over the next 30 years he devoted his military career to defeating the Mitanni
  • By 1350, he had finally defeated the Mitanni and now controlled northern Syria
  • He made his sons Telipinus and Piyassilis as kings of Aleppo and Carchemish
  • This brought the Hittite kingdom to Imperial power and their height
