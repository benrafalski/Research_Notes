February 15, 2024

Hospital Raid

  • IDF raids Nasser hospital in Rafah
  • IDF said raid is "precise and limited"
  • IDF says Hamas was in hospital, Hamas denies
  • Displaced Pals flee to Rafah city and Dier Al-Balah
  • Medical charity Medicins San Frontieres says IDF shelled hospital before, despite telling patients to remain
  • IDF apprehends various suspect within hospital
  • IDF Hagari says objective was to ensure hospital could continue running
  • GHM says IDF forces doctors from ICU patiets
  • IDF says they killed 15 Hamas militants in Gaza and Khan Younis in last 24-hours

Lebanon Strikes

  • Hezbollah says Israel will "pay the price" for killing 10 civilians, including 5 children
  • IDF says they were responding to rocket fire on border town Safed that killed one IDF soldier
  • IDF says they killed two commanders and another operative of Hezbollah Radwan unit in Nabatieh
  • Nabatieh is dense urban area, far from border
  • Hezbollah says three of its fighters were killed
  • Two total strikes
  • First strike killed a woman and 2 children in al-Sawana village near border
  • Second strike killed 7 civilians with 3 children in Nabatieh
  • Both sides say they do not want all out war in Lebanon
