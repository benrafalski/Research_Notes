Paleolithic and Mesolithic Periods

  • Earliest found human remains are from the Middle Paleolithic Period around 700,000 years ago
  • By the Mesolithic Period, Natufian culture began to spread through the Levant (Syria and Palestine)
  • The Natufians fished, hunted, and gathered grains
  • They eventually domesticated animals, began to produce pottery, and cultivated single-grained wheat
  • By 4000 BC, Syrians began to produce bronze and shortly after began to build cities
