Assyrians

  • Located between northern Iraq and southeastern Turkey
  • During most of the 2000s BC they were dependent on Balylonia and then Mitanni
  • Became an independent state during early 1400s BC
  • In 1365 BC, Ashur-uballit I came to power and transformed Assyria into an empire
  • He defeated the Mitanni with the help of the Hittites
  • He then defeated the Hitites and Hurrians with his ally the Kassites
  • In 1243 BC Tukulti-Ninurta came to power and defeated the Kassites, holding Babylonia for a time
  • Their power declined in 1208 BC after Tukulti-Ninurta I was slain by his own son
