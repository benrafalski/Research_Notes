Great Syrian Revolt

Jabal al-Druze Revolt

  • In 1925, local grievances sparked a revolt to break out in the Jabal al-Druze State
  • In October 1925, the rebels entered the city of Damasucs resulting in a two day bombardment by the French
  • The revolt would continue until 1927, however the French had gained abck some control by the end of 1925

The People's Party

  • The Druze rebels and nationalists of Damascus eventually joined together in the People's Party

End of revolution and Aftermath

  • In 1928, new elections were held for a Constituent Assembly
  • The Syrian nationalists won the elections and formed an assembly which drafter a constitution
  • However, the constitution was not accepted by the high commissioner due to Syrian unification and didn't include the French position of control
