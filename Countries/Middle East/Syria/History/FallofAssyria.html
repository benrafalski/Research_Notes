Fall of Assyria

  • Between 800-700 BC, there was a large movement of people to the south
  • By 700 BC, the Assyrians began to be invaded by the Cimmerians and Scythians
  • Cimmerians: forces from Russia by the Scythians to Anatolia and then expelled again by Sargon II and were defeated in late 600s
  • Scythinas: From Iran, went to Russia/Ukraine and established an empire in Crimea and were eventually defeated by Sarmatians in 400-200 BC period
  • In 612 BC, the Assyrians were finally defeated by the Iranian Medes
