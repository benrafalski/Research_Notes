Hyksos

  • During 1800 BC, Semitic migration out of Syria into Norther Egypt began
  • By 1674 BC, they had regal control over north Egypt
  • This led to the formation of the Hyksos dynasty in 1630 BC
  • This dynasty brought new technologies like the horse chariot, compound bow, and new metal weapons
  • They would eventually fall in 1523 BC
