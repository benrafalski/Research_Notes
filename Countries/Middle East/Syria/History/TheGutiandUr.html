The Guti and Ur

  • The Guti were from the Zagros mountains in Iran and overthrew Naram-Sin and the Akkad dynasty
  • Not much is known about them and they ruled until 2130 BC
  • In 2130 BC they were overthrow by Utu-khegal of Uruk
  • Utu-khegal established the 3rd dynasty of Ur
  • Not much is known about Ur rule in Syria
  • Ur power in Syria was eventually ended by the Amorites (westerners) migration to Syria
