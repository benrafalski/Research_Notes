Nebuchadnezzar II

  • In 605 BC, the king of Egypt Necho II, tried to rescue the Assyrians
  • However, his army was destroyed by Nebuchadnezzar II, the king of the Chaldean dynasty of Babylonia
  • In 597, he captured Jerusalem and sent the Jews into exile
  • For the following 50 years, Syria would be ruled by the throne of Babylon
