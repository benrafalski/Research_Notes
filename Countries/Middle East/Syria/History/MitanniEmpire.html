Mitanni Empire

  • Indo-Iranian state that was founded in 1500 BC
  • The Indo-Iranians came from Aryan tribes and migrated to Mesopotamia, settling with the Hurrian peoples of the Tigris
  • They would soon become a noble class called maryannu
  • The Mitanni would long be the dominant empire in Syria
  • By 1360 BC, they were overthrown by the Hittite king Suppiluliumas I
  • The Matannis would later become part of the Hittite empire, being called Hanigalbat
