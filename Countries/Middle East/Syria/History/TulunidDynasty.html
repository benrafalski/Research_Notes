Tulunid Dynasty

  • During Abbasid rule, Syria began to drift from Baghdad's control
  • In 868, Ahmad ibn Tulun, founded the Tulunid dynasty in Egypt
  • In 877, Syria was annexed by the Tulunids
  • After Tulun died, the Tulunids fell into decline and eventully Syria returned to the Abbasids
