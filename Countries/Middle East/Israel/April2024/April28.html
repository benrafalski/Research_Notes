US assessment on Israel for NSM-20

  • Leaked internal State Department memo shows some senior US officials advised Blinken they think Israel is violating international humanitarian law
  • Joint submissio from four bureaus - Democracy Human Rights & Labor; Population, Refugees and Migration; Global Criminal Justice and International Organization Affairs
  • The join submission found Israel's assurances were "neither credible nor reliable" and cited 8 examples of when the IDF potentially violated IHL
  • Also site 11 instances of IDF "arbitrarily restricting humanitarian aid"
  • Conversely, a separate submission from the bureau of Political and Military Affairs said a suspension of US arms sales would invite "provications" by Iran

Ceasefire talks and Rafah assault

  • Israel foreign minister Israel Katz says Rafah incursion will be suspended if there is a hostage deal
  • Turkey aid flotilla headed for Gaza halted after Guinea Bissau removed its flags from two vessels (flotilla had three vessels total)
