April 14, 2024

Iran launches drone attack on Israel

  • Iran launches more than 300 drones and ground-to-ground missiles at Israel
  • Marks first time Iran directly targeted Israel, rather than using their proxies
  • One IDF military facility reportedly damaged, total damage from attack reported to be only modest
  • Majority of the drones/missiles reportedly taken down by US/Israeli/British/Jordanian air defenses, mostly over Syrian airspace
  • Israel and Jordan close airspace
  • Joe Biden convenes G7 meeting
  • Biden tells Netanyahu he would oppose an Israeli counterattack against Iran but pledges to support Israel
  • Iran says they have no plans for a follow-up attack
  • Two senior Israeli ministers signal that a retaliation by Israel is no imminent, Israel would not act alone
  • Condemned attack: European Union, Britain, Canada, Germany, Argentina, Paraguay, Chile, Czech Republic, Denmark, France, Mexico, the Netherlands and Norway
  • Supported attack: Hezbollah, Houthis, some Gazan Pals, Hamas, PIJ, Palestinian Popular Resistance Committee (PRC)
  • Small gatherings reported inside Iran, celebrating attack

Cairo Talks

  • Hamas rejects Israel ceasefire response
  • Main Hamas demands remain unchanged, demands agreement must permanently end the war
