April 9, 2024

Cairo Talks

  • Hamas says Israeli ceasefire proposal meets none of their demands
  • Hamas wants a permanent ceasefire and Israel to fully withdraw from Gaza
  • Israel wants their hostages back and to eradicate Hamas
  • Israel says they would free Pal prisoners in exchange for hostages but will not end their military campaign

Turkey

  • Turkey imposed export restrictions on Israel until Gaza ceasefire
  • First significant measure against Israel by Turkey since start of war
  • Restictions include curbs on steel, fertilizer, jet fuel, and more
  • Restrictions follow Israel rejection of a Turkish request for air-drop operation into Gaza
  • Restrictions follow growing domestic criticism of Erdogan for continuing ties with Israel

Israel Aid to Gaza

  • Netanyahu says Israel will eliminate Hamas in Rafah, says nothing will stop them
  • Israel purchases 40,000 tents for Gazans ahead of Rafah evacuations
  • IDF says 4 Hamas battalions remain in Rafah
  • Israel says 419 trucks entered on Monday 4/8
