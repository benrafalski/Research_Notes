April 24, 2024

Lebanon fighting

  • Escalation of violence between Israel and Hezbollah continues
  • Hezbollah volleys rockets into Israel and Israel responds with heavy shelling in Lebanon border town

Gaza shelling and Rafah

  • Israel obtains 40,000 tents (each hold 10 people) ahead of Rafah evacuation
  • Israel begins setting up tent encampments in Khan Younis
  • Evacuation plans to be finalized in the next two weeks, evacuation will take around a month
  • Four Hamas battalions are said to be stationed in Rafah
  • Pal civilians in norther Gaza continue to flee their homes due to increased IDF shelling at Beit Lahiya (IDF gave evacuation orders there 4 days ago)
  • IDF says they were shelling Beit Lahiya since that is where the PIJ rockets from 4/23 had been fired from

International developments

  • Iran, Pakistan call on UNSC to take action on Israel for "illegally" targeting neighbouring countries and foreign diplomatic facilities
  • US campus protests spread into the streets of Brooklyn, university protests escalate further, spreading to Berkeley in California
