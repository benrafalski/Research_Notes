April 23, 2024

Gaza and Lebanon escalations

  • IDF strikes intensify accross Gaza
  • IDF orders new evacuations from the north of the strip
  • PIJ launches rocket attack from Gaza into Israel, no casualties reported
  • Spokesperson for Hamas calls for an escalation in conflict on all fronts and praised Iran
  • Total bodies collected hits 330 from reported "mass graves" at Nasser hospital
  • Israel says they were forced to dig up bodies already buried there to check for dead hostages and that Hamas was operating at Nasser
  • Hezbollah launches drone attack on Acre IDF base, deepest attack since start of Gaza war, no damage reported
  • IDF kills two Hezbollah fighers in Southern Lebanon, brings total to 270 militants killed and 50 civilians

UNRWA Colonna Review

  • UN review by former French foreign minister Catherine Colonna released on UNRWA neutrality
  • Review says Israel has still not provided evidence that "over 450 UNRWA staff" are terrorists
  • Could lead to review of funding for countries that previously froze funding
  • Colonna review also found "neutrality-related issues persist" among UNRWA
  • Said some staff publicly expressed political views, problematic textbooks being used in schools, and politicized staff unions

US Protests

  • Police arrest dozens at pro-Palestinian demonstrations at Yale and New York University
  • Columbia University cancels in-person classes in response to protesters setting up tent encampments on campus
  • Biden calls the demonstrations antisemetic
