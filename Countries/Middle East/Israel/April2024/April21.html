April 21, 2024

WB Violence

  • IDF kills 14 Pals during raid in Nur Shams area (near Tulkarm) in the WB
  • Pal militants the Tulkarm Brigades say they exchanged fire with the IDF
  • IDF says they killed a number of militants in the raid
  • Pal health ministry says ambulance drive was killed following settler attack from April 12 after Israeli teen was found dead
  • 21 total houses reported burned down following settler attack as well
  • IDF also says they shot dead two Pals who tried to stab them in Hebron

Israel political developments

  • Thousands protest in Israel demanding new elections and for more to be done to get the hostages back
  • Israel indicts the sister of Hamas leader Ismail Haniyeh for incitement and showing solidarity with a terror group, after she allegedly praised Hamas' Oct. 7 attack
  • After Oct 7 Haniyeh sent messages to dozens of contacts, praising the assult and calling for further "slaughter"
  • On message read: "Oh God, count them and kill them and don't leave any of them, Oh God."
  • Netanyahu says he will fight against sanctions from Washington after reports that the US was planning to sanction some IDF battalions for alleged rights violations
  • Axios reported US planned to sanction Israel's Netzah Yehuda battalion that has operated in the occupied West Bank.
  • Netzah Yehuda battalion was set up in 1999 for ultra-Orthodix jews to be able to serve and still practice their religious practices
  • Netzah Yehuda soldiers were accused of being involved in the death of a 78-year-old Palestinian-American, Omar Assad
  • Omar Assad died of a heart attack in 2022 after being detained and was later found abandoned at a building site
  • Autopsy found Assad died from a stress-induced heart attack brought on by being manhandled
  • Result was the battalion commander being reprimanded and two officers being dismissed but no criminal charges
