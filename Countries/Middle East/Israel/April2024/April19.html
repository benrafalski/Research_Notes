Israel attack on Tehran

  • Netanyahu had initially approved plan to strike on 4/15 but held back
  • Explosions heard over Isfahan around Iranian Air Force
  • Sources report that Israel was behind the attack
  • Iran downplayed incident and say they have no plan to retaliate

  • US says they were not involved in incident
  • Iran officials avoid mentioning Israel in attacks, say drones were from "infiltrators from inside Iran"
  • Experts see response to the attack as a de-escalation
