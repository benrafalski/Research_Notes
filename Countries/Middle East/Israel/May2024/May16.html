May 16, 2024

Gaza/WB fighting

  • Fighting continues in Jabalia and Rafah
  • Three Pals killed, dozens arrested in IDF raids in Ramallah, Hebron, Jenin, Nablus, Tubas and Qalqilya in the WB
  • Raids target money changing shops and IDF said to have confiscated large sums of money
  • Defense Minister Gallant publicly calls out Netanyahu for not having a clear day after plan once Hamas is destroyed, says Israel cannot militarily occupy Gaza and a non-hostile Palestinian government must be set up
  • Five IDF soldiers killed by IDF friendly tank fire in north Gaza

Rafah Crossing

  • Egypt rejects Israeli request for Rafah crossing to reopen, says Israel just wants to push all the Pals into the Sinai
  • Egypt's Pres. al-Sisi says Israel continues to evade efforts to reach a ceasefire deal with Hamas

US Pier

  • US anchors aid pier to Gaza beach
  • Still no plan in place for effective aid distribution, which has been the main problem during the war
