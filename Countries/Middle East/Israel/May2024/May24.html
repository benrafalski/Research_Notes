May 24, 2024

ICJ Ruling

  • South Africa requested ICJ rule on Rafah in request in May 17
  • ICJ rules that Israel must immediatley halt its military assault on Rafah in emergency ruling
  • Ruling is binding but ICJ has no means of enforcing the ruling
  • "The state of Israel shall (....) immediately halt its military offensive, and any other action in the Rafah governorate, which may inflict on the Palestinian group in Gaza conditions of life that could bring about its physical destruction in whole or in part"
  • Also orders Israel to open the Rafah crossing between Egypt and Gaza to let in aid
  • Demands Israel report back on their progress in Gaza within one month
  • Panel of 15 international judges ruled 13-2, Uganda and Israel itself voted against
  • South Africa hailed the ruling as groundbreaking
  • Hamas calls on UNSC to enforce the ruling
  • Israel's opposition leader Lapid says the order was "a moral collapse and a moral disaster"
  • Israel signals they will ignore the ruling and continue Rafah operation

Gaza war

  • Strikes in south and central Rafah following ICJ ruling
  • Israeli strikes continue in Jabalia
  • Israel recovers the bodies of three hostages in Jabalia that were killed on October 7
  • Hostages recovered include Hanan Yablonka, Michel Nisenbaum (killed at Nova) and Orion Hernandez Radoux (Shani Louk BF, killed at Nova)
