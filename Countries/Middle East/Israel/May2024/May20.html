May 20, 2024

ICC arrest warrants

  • ICC prosecutor Karim Khan requests arrest warrants for Israel and Hamas leaders accusing them of war crimes
  • Says all accused "bear criminal responsibility" for alleged war crimes and crimes against humanity
  • Israeli leaders include: PM Benjamin Netanyahu and Defense Minister Yoav Gallant
  • Hamas leaders include: Hamas leader Yahya Sinwar, commander-in-chief of the Hamas military wing (Qassam brigades) Mohammed Al-Masri (known as Deif), and head of Hamas political bureau Ismail Haniyeh
  • Israel accusations include: Says Netanyahu and Gallant systematically deprived civilians of "objects indispensable to human survival," including restricting food, water, medicine and energy
  • Hamas accusations include: Says Hamas leader are responsible for crimes committed by Hamas including extermination and murder, the taking of hostages, torture, rape and other acts of sexual violence.
  • A panel of pre-trial judges will determine if the evidence supports the issuing of the arrest warrants
  • ICC has no power to enforce arrest warrants
  • Israel is not a member of the ICC (along with US, Russia, and China)
  • Both Israeli and Hamas leaders respond by denouncing Karim Khan's move

  • Israel's foreign minister, Israel Katz calls for support from the US against the establishment of a Palestinian state
