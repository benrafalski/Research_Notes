May 10, 2024

Rafah

  • IDF continues fighting in eastern Rafah with Hamas and PIJ militants, IDF says they have killed 50 militants in east Rafah so far and uncovered several tunnels
  • Truce talks end with no deal after Hamas says they will no accept any concessions from their previous deal
  • With truce talk failure, Israel says they will continue with Rafah assault
  • Netanyahu says "If we must, we shall fight with our fingernails," in response to Biden withholding aid
  • US pause does not include all weapons aid to Israel, military aid already in the approval pipline are still being delivered
  • Rep. Gregory Meeks pauses $18 billion in separate US pause to review more info about how Israel will use them

UN Developments

  • UNRWA headquarters closes after being set on fire by Israeli arsonists two times while a crowd cheered in East Jerusalem
  • UN aid chief says nothing allowed into or out of Gaza for three days
  • UN General Assembly votes to make Palestine able to qualify for full UN membership
  • UNSC to vote again on if Palestine will actually become a full UN member, US likely to veto
  • US law prevents US from funding any UN org that grants full membership to a group that doesn't have internationally recongnized state already.
