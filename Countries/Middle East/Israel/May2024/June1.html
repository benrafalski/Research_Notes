June 1, 2024

  • Biden presents three phase proposal from Israel to Hamas for a ceasefire in Gaza
  • Netanyahu office says they back the plan
  • Netanyahu also says there will be no permanent ceasefire unless Hamas is destroyed, casting doubt on the Biden plan
  • Qatar relayed the proposal to Hamas and said they responded "positively"
  • IDF probes further into Rafah

Israel-Biden Truce Proposal

Phase 1

  • Last 6 weeks
  • "Full and complete" ceasefire
  • Withdraw of IDF forces from "all polulated areas"
  • "Release of a number of hostages including women, the elderly, the wounded in exchange for release of hundreds of Palestinian prisoners."
  • Return of all Gazans to their homes/neighborhoods in all areas of Gaza
  • Allow for 600 trucks of humanitarian aid into Gaza everyday
  • Talks between Israel and Hamas on the next stage of the proposal

Phase 2

  • "A permanent end to hostilities."
  • Will only begin after the first phase negotiations are done
  • If phase 1 takes longer than 6 weeks, the ceasefire will be extended
  • Release of all remaining hostages that are alive, including male soldiers
  • All IDF forces to withdraw from Gaza
  • Biden: "And as long as Hamas lives up to its commitments, a temporary ceasefire will become - in the words of the Israeli proposal - the cessation of hostilities permanently."

Phase 3

  • "A major reconstruction plan for Gaza would commence and any final remains of hostages who have been killed would be returned to their families."
  • Biden: "At this point, Hamas no longer is capable of carrying out another Oct. 7."
  • Reconstruction of Gaza with the help of Arab nations and the international community in a "manner that does not allow Hamas to rearm"
