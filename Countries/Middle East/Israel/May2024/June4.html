June 4, 2024

  • Shas, ultra-Orthodox Jewish party (11/120 seats in parliament), back Biden-Israel truce deal in statement
  • Jewish Power party (Ben-Gvir) and Religious Zionism party (Smotrich) control 13 seats and still refuse to back the deal
  • 40% of Israelis support the ceasefire proposal, 27% are opposed, and 33% undecided
  • Iran backed Shia militias in Iraq ramp up missile and rocket attacks on Israel
  • Groups include Kataib Hezbollah and Nujaba and have turned their attention from the US to Israel following the US retaliation following the Jordan strike that killed 3 US service members
  • Houthi attacks decline according to Greece due to international naval group Aspides and US-led Operation Prosperity Guardian to counter missile and drone attacks
  • UN human rights chief expresses support fo the Biden truce deal
