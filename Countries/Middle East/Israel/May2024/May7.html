May 7, 2024

Hamas ceasefire proposal

  • Hamas accepts Qatari-Egytian ceasefire proposal
  • Israel says the current Hamas proposal was unacceptable to Israel
  • Proposal returns 18 in first phase and the rest of the 130 hostages in later phases in exchange for Pal prisoners
  • Proposal would not allow for Israel to veto Pal prisoner demands, including Fatah leader Marwan Barghouti (life sentence for deadly terrorist attacks)
  • Proposal calls for a lift on dual-use items from Gaza blockade
  • US is reviewing the Hamas accepted proposal
  • Biden phone call with Netanyahu, reiterates his position on Rafah invasion
  • Qatar mediators and mid-level Israeli team to head to Cairo for further negotiations

Rafah invasion

  • Israeli military takes over operational control of the Pal side of Gaza's Rafah border crossing
  • IDF shuts down Rafah aid route at Rafah crossing
  • Kerem Shalom crossing remains closed too following Hamas attacks on the aid route
  • IDF says they killed 20 militants and found three tunnel shafts in eastern Rafah incursion
