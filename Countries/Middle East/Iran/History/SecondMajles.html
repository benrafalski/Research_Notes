Second Majles

  • After Mohammad Ali Shah took over the Majles in 1908 a civil war broke out with the Cossack Brigade fighting on his side.
  • He ended up losing in 1909 and fled to Russia.
  • The second Majles was erected in November 1909 and the 11 year old Ahmad Shah took over the throne.
