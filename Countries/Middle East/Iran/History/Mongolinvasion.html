Mongol invasion

  • In 1215, Genghis Khan conquered China and was looking west for trade routes.
  • Khan's first two missions to Khwarezm ended in his men being massacred and the only option left was war.
  • In 1220, Genghis Khan conquered Transoxania and slaughtered the populations in its cities
  • Ala al-Din fled and left his son, Jalal al-Din, as the Khwarezm-Shah.
  • Jalal al-Din was murdered in 1231 after the Mongols defeated the Khwarezm-Shahs
  • Mongol rule left Iran divided.
