Turkmen

  • From 1375-1468 the Kara Koyunlu (Black sheep), descendants of the Jalayirids, dominated western Iran in Azerbaijan.
  • In 1402 Timur gave northern Iraq to Kara Osman, the leader of the Kara Koyunlu rival, Ak Koyunlu (white sheep).
  • By 1467, Ak Koyunlu, now led by Uzun Hasan, defeated the Kara Koyunlu and took over west Iran and Azerbaijan
  • By 1469 he controlled all of Iran
  • Uzun Hasan was eventually defeated by the Ottoman Turks and his son Ya'qub supplanted him
  • Ya'qub tried to revive Sunni values in the area but failed and only gave the incentive for Shi'ism to spread.
