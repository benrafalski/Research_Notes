Qajar Dynasty

  • Under Karim Khan's rule he imprisoned the Qajar leader, Agha Muhammad Khan Qajar, because of his repeated attempts to take power.
  • In 1779, when Karim Khan died, Agha Mohammad escaped.
  • In 1786, Tehran was made the capital of Iran and Agha Mohammad was seen as the top political leader.
  • In 1796, he conquered Georgia and took over Khorasan.
  • Agha Mohammad built Tehran from a village to a city under his rule and ruled with an iron fist.
  • In 1797, Agha Mohammad was assassinated and supplanted by his nephew, Fath Ali Shah.
