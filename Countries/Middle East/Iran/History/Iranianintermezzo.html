Iranian intermezzo

  • In 821, Tahir ibn al-Husayn established the Tahirid dynasty in Khorasan and was the start of Persian independence
  • The dynasty was loyal to the Abbasid caliphate but enjoyed virtual independence.
  • The Abbasid caliphate had trouble in Khorasan and felt it was easier to appoint a great general to govern the area instead
  • Tahir would die soon after being appointed but his family would still rule until Yaâ€™qub overthrew them.
