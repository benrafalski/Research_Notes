  • He ruled over a period of peace.
  • In 1779, he died and a civil war broke out between the Zands and the Qajar.
  • From 1779-1789 five different Zand kings ruled.
  • In 1789, Lotf Ali Khan Zand took the throne.
  • Over the next few years, the Zands had control of little territory
  • By 1794, the Qajar forces broke the Zand line and captured and killed Lotf Ali Khan.
