Khwarezm-Shahs

  • In 1077 Anustegin Gharacha'i was appointed governor of Khwarezm by Seljuq sultan Malik-Shah and founded the Khwarezm-Shah dynasty.
  • After Seljuq rule ended in 1153 there was a brief period of independence
  • Evantually, the Eldeguzid and Salghurid dynasties rose in Azerbaijan and Fars, splitting Iran
  • After 1157 when sultan Sanjar died, there was a power struggle in Iran
  • Ala al-Din Muhammad led the Khwarezm-Shahs to victory and controlled a majority of Iran by 1200
  • Ala al-Din did not like the Abbasid caliph and even set up his own anti caliph in opposition
