Abbasid caliphate

  • After the Umayyads were overthrown the Abbasid caliphate rose to power in Baghdad
  • The dynasty was Islamic and made up of both Arab and Persian people
  • Stretched from Egypt to Iran
  • They were not expansionist like the Umayyad dynasty
  • Rebellions formed as peasant revolts in Azerbaijan and Khorasan
  • During this time many began to move to Sistan in Iran including the Kharijites
  • Eventually the different factions in Sistan were united under Ya'qub ibn Layth al-Saffar who would later form the Saffarid dynasty.
