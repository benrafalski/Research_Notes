Sir Frank Swettenham

  • In 1882, he became the advider to Selangor
  • Promoted coffee/tobacco estates and boosted tin earnings by building a railway from Kuala Lumpur to Klang port.
  • He also was the adviser to Perak
  • In 1895, he made an agreement with Perak, Selangor, Negri Sembilan, and Pahang to form the Malay federation
  • In 1896, became first adviser-general of the Malay federation
  • Malay federation consisted of Perak, Selangor, Negeri Sembilan, and Pahang
  • Kuala Lumpur was the capital
