Labuan joins North Borneo

  • After the end of WWII, the North Borneo Company's rule ended
  • North Borneo was turned into a crown colony with Labuan being incorporated
  • On July 15, 1946 Labuan joined North Borneo
  • The capital of Labuan, Victoria, was rebuilt during this time after being destroyed by Japan during WWII
  • The capital was moved from Sandakan to Jesselton (modern Kota Kinabalu)
  • The crown colony soon rebuilt its economy due to rubber and tin produciton
  • During the 1950s, the Kadazan community began to gain popularity with the spread of the radio and newspapers
