Federation of Malaya

  • After the strikes and demonstrations broke out, the UMNO and British began to negotiate a new solution
  • Between June-December 1946, the British and Malays negotiated the Malaya Agreement at the Pleno Conference
  • In February 1948, the agreement was signed by the British governemnt, creating the Federation of Malaya
  • The federation unified the Malay territories, but gave special rights to the Malay nationals, including the position of newly reinstated sultan
  • These developments would lead the radical/poor Chinese to lead an insurgency known as the Malayan Emergency
  • States included: Johor, Kedah, Kelantan, Malacca, Negeri Sembilan, Pahang, Penang, Perak, Perlis, Selangor, and Terengganu
  • Executive branch: led by British High Commissionser (first was Sir Edward Gent) assisted by the Federation of Malaya Executive council
  • Legislature: Federation of Malaya Legislative Council
