Malaysia Agreement

  • In 1961, the UN made a committe called the Decolonialisation Committee, to help states reach Independence
  • In November 1961, this led to the Malaysia Agreement being signed
  • Under the agreement, the UK would give up Singapore, Sarawak, and North Borneo
  • The SUPP party of Sarawak came out agaist the Federation and wanted Sarawak to be independent on its own
  • They would combine with the Federation of Malaya to form Malaysia
  • A representative from Brunei was also sent but they ended up not signing it because the Brunei sultan was not getting recognized as the sole ruler
