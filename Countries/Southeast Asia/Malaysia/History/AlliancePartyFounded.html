Alliance Party Founded

  • In 1952, the Alliance Party was originally founded by the UMNO and MCA for the Kuala Lumpur elections
  • The Alliance was led by the UMNO who was led by Tunku Abdul Rahman Putra Alhaj
  • The coalition ended up winning 9/12 seats during that election
  • As the coalition grew stonger, in 1954, they were joined my the MIC
  • In 1955, the first general election was held for the Federal Legislative Council (parliment) and the Alliance won 51/52 of the seats
  • By October 1957, the Alliance was formally registered as a political organiation
  • After Malaysia got independence, the first general election in 1959 resulted in the Alliance winning 74/104 seats
  • In 1963, the Alliance was credited with securing the formation of the Federation of Malaysia
  • Following the formation of the new Federation, the Alliance grew closer with parties in Sarawak and Sabah
  • In the 1964 elections, due to popularity for the formation of Malaysia, the Alliance won 84/104 seats
  • In 1969, the Alliance won their most seats but the results of the election led to the May 13 riots and a state of emergency
  • In 1971, the Malaysian Parliament reconvened and by 1972 former opposition parties such as the DAP and PMIP joined the Alliance
  • In 1973, this resulted in the Alliance being replaced by the 9 party coaliton called Barisan Nasional
