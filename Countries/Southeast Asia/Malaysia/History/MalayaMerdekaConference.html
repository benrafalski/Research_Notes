Malaya Merdeka Conference

  • In January 1956, Tunku Abdul Rahman led a Malaya mission to London called the Merdeka Conference
  • The goal of the mission was to negotiate for independence
  • The negotiations were a success an in February 1956, the Treaty of London was signed to set up an independent Federation of Malaya
  • The independent federation was to be set up by August 1957
