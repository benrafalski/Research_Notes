First Sarawak Elections

  • In June 1959, the Sarawak United People's Party (SUPP) was founded to organize a political front to gain independence
  • The SUPP is a multiracial center-right political party today but originally had ties to communism
  • In November 1959, Sarawak had their first district council elections
  • The elections were to elect 24 members to the Council Negri (Sarawak State Legislative Assembly)
  • Following the elections, the Parti Negara Sarawak (PANAS) was founded in April 1960
