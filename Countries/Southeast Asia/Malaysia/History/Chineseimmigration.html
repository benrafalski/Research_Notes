Chinese immigration

  • During the 1800s, the Chinese began to immigrate to Malaysia and settled in the weaker sultanates (not Johor) to escape poverty at home.
  • They formed tight communities and often mined tin.
  • Some groups fought wars against each other and the Chinese did not integrate fully with the Malays, increasing friction between the ethnicities.
  • They established Kuala Lumpur and Ipoh.
