Sulu ceded to Phillipines

  • In March 1940, the first lady of Sulu, Dayang Dayang relinqushed her claims against the Government of Philippines
  • On April 4 1940, she renounced her claim against the Philippine Government over the Sultanate of Sulu
  • This ceded the Sulu archipelago to the Philippines but she continued to claim North Borneo.
