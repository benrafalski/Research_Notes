British colonial expansion

  • Brits were initially adverse to the Saimese
  • Sweetenham convinced them to take control of the states by painting the native rulers as bad and warning against rival European intervention.
  • In 1909, the Brits gained control over northern Malay from the Saim (Thai)
  • Added Kedah, Terengganu, Kelantan, and Perlis to their colonial rule
  • None of them became part of the Malay federation yet though
