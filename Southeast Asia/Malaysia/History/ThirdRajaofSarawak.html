Third Raja of Sarawak

  • In 1917, Charles Vyner de Windt Brooke succeeded his father to become the third raja
  • He would be the final "white raja"
  • After WWI ended, there was a rubber and oil boom that expanded the Sarawak economy
  • This led Brooke to begin modernizing Sarawak
  • Public services were developed, a British India law code was enstated, and education was expanded
