UMNO Founded

  • During the 1940s many naationalist convervative westernized elite Malays began to form Malay Associations (voluntary organizations) around the country
  • In March 1946, 41 of the associations formed the United Malays National Organisation (UMNO)
  • The party formed to assert dominance over Malaysia, after the British proposed the Malaysian Union following WWII
