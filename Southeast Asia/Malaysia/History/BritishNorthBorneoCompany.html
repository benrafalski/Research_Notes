British North Borneo Company

  • By 1881, the British had obtained so much territory in North Borneo they launched the British North Borneo Company
  • The British North Borneo Company was based in Sandakan
  • It ruled the British protectorate of North Borneo until 1941
  • The company was operated for the intrest of the shareholders and was not very successfull
  • However, modern Sabah still uses the same administrative, economic, and political system it established
